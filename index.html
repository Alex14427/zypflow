FAQ

Common questions

How long does setup take?
Most businesses are fully set up in 15-30 minutes. You paste one line of code on your website for the chat widget, connect your calendar, and tell the AI about your services. Our onboarding wizard guides you through every step. No developers needed.
Will the AI sound robotic to my customers?
Not at all. Zypflow uses the latest GPT-4o and Claude models trained specifically for service business conversations. You customise the tone, and customers routinely cannot tell they are speaking with AI. The AI identifies itself as an assistant when asked directly.
Can I try it before committing?
Yes. Every plan includes a 14-day free trial with full access — no credit card required. You can test the AI receptionist, booking system, and reminders with real customers before paying anything.
What happens if the AI can't answer a question?
If the AI encounters a question outside its knowledge base, it gracefully collects the customer's details and escalates to you via SMS or email. You always have full control, and can jump into any conversation in real time from the dashboard.
Is my data secure and GDPR compliant?
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We are fully GDPR compliant with data processing agreements, and your business data is never used to train AI models. Data is stored on UK/EU servers. You can export or delete all data at any time.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. There are no contracts, no lock-in periods, and no cancellation fees. You can cancel from your dashboard with one click. Your data remains available for 30 days after cancellation in case you change your mind.
